Find your amoeba today :)

A quiz website to sharpen your skills, and own a round squishy amoeba as your very own virtual pet. Practice your general knowledge with fast paced quizzes, and earn points to buy accessories for your amoeba!

What is an amoeba?

An amoeba is a happy, bouncy creature. It loves being fed with knowledge, and enjoys wearing hats even if they may not fit very well on it's round physique. It's here to cheer you on and keep you company as you train hard and beat your quiz scores. Earn points by doing more quizzes to buy your amoeba accessories, and watch it become even happier!

How do the quizzes work?

Sign up to become a member at Amoeba, and you'll gain access to a vast variety of quizzes. These quizzes are designed to test your general knowledge and speed. You'll only be given 5 seconds to answer each question. Each quiz is repeatable, so practice until you can get all the questions right. When you finish a quiz, the score given will be added to your total score. You'll be displayed on a leaderboard with other members,ranked by your total score. Have the highest total score out of everybody else and you'll be first!
